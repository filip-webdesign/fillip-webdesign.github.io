Being Croatian in America

In the land of burgers and fries, I aspire to be,
A Croatian student, playing soccer for free.
With skill in my feet, and love for the game,
I dribble past attackers they will never be same!
But the confusion, from people I meet,
they think I'm speaking gibberish, its quite a feat.
I Shout Hvala for thanks, and Dobar dan for hi,
They scratch their heads, wondering why.
Here is the end of my inspiration,
I hope it is fine.
being Croatian in America is not easy,
Im not going to lie!
